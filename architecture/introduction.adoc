= Introduction & Goals

Bandcheck's mission statement is to make discovering music safer. Our service delivers
tools where the community contributes to safer listening experience by contributing to
a shared knowledge base of content warnings.
Reviews are evidence based and focus on content warnings rather than subjective ratings.
This empowers users to make informed decisions about the music they choose to listen to.

The whole platform is non-profit and open source. We believe that access to information about
potentially triggering content should be freely available to everyone.

== Requirements

The main requirements driving the architecture of Bandcheck are:

Quorum Based Contributions::
  Reviews and content warnings are contributed by the community. The architecture must
  support a robust system for user contributions, including mechanisms for moderation
  and validation to ensure the accuracy and reliability of the information provided.

Discovery and Content Warnings::
  The platform must provide efficient search and discovery features, allowing users to
  easily find music along with relevant content warnings. This includes advanced filtering
  options based on user preferences and warning types.

No Registration Required::
  Social logins are the main authentication mechanism. Users should be able to access
  the platform and its features without mandatory registration, ensuring ease of access
  while still allowing for personalized experiences through optional sign-ins.

== Quality Goals

Cost Efficiency::
  The architecture should be designed to minimize operational costs, leveraging
  cloud services and open-source technologies where possible to reduce expenses
  associated with hosting, data storage, and content delivery.

Maintainability::
  We aim to use widely adopted technologies and frameworks to ensure that the codebase
  is maintainable and can be easily understood and contributed to by new developers.

Operability::
  The platform follows ux best practices to ensure a smooth and intuitive user experience.

== Stakeholders

Core Maintainers::
  Responsible for the overall development and maintenance of the Bandcheck platform.

Community Contributors::
    Users who contribute reviews and content warnings, playing a crucial role in enriching the platform's knowledge base and ensuring diverse perspectives are represented.

End Users::
    Individuals who utilize the Bandcheck platform to discover music and access content warnings, benefiting from the community-driven insights provided.
